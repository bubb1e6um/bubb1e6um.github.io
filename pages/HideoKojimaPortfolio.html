Metal Gear Series

Metal Gear (Japanese: METAL GEARメタルギア, Hepburn: Metaru Gia) is a franchise of action-adventure stealth games. Developed and published by Konami, the first game, Metal Gear, was released in 1987 for MSX home computers. The player often takes control of a special forces operative, usually Solid Snake, who is assigned the task of finding the titular superweapon, "Metal Gear", a bipedal walking tank with the ability to launch nuclear weapons.

Death Stranding Series

A fractured world on the brink of oblivion, where invisible strands tether life, death, and hope. From the solitary footsteps of Sam Porter Bridges to Fragile's burden across surreal beaches, this saga unravels the fragile connections that bind humanity through time, loss, and sacrifice. In this world, connection is survival — and survival is a story yet unfinished.

About me

I am a game designer based in Japan. Has a 40 years experience .

I do not simply make games. I create worlds. My work has always been about breaking the borders between film, literature, music, and interactive art. Each project I direct is both a story and an experience — a way to connect players emotionally to the screen while making them question the reality beyond it.
